# Paloma365 CRM & Loyalty — Program Guide

## What the module does

CRM & Loyalty builds unified guest profiles from every order channel and
runs configurable loyalty mechanics on top: cashback, bonus points,
tiered discounts and birthday rewards. It is the primary tool for lifting
guest retention and repeat visit share.

## Guest profiles

Every order — dine-in, delivery or takeaway — is linked to a guest by
phone number. The profile keeps visit history, favourite dishes, average
ticket and channel preferences. Profiles merge automatically when a guest
uses a new phone number with the same payment card.

## Loyalty mechanics

Cashback accrues as bonus points, configurable per category: for example
5% on the main menu and 10% on the lunch menu. Points expire after a
configurable period (90 days by default) to encourage return visits.
Tiered programs (Silver/Gold/Platinum) unlock higher accrual rates at
spending thresholds.

## Campaigns

The campaign builder segments guests by RFM (recency, frequency, monetary)
and sends targeted offers. Typical campaigns: win-back for guests inactive
30+ days, birthday offers with a 7-day validity window, and double-points
happy hours to fill weak dayparts. SMS delivery is billed separately by
the telecom provider; push notifications through the guest app are free.

## Expected impact

Venues that launch a loyalty program with at least one active campaign
typically see retention improve within two to three months. The module's
uplift assumptions used in ROI projections are deliberately conservative:
about four percent order growth and two percent average-ticket growth.
